About GreenAgritrade

The objective of GreenAgriTrade is to create a user-friendly and efficient trading platform where farmers, merchants, and administrators can interact seamlessly. Farmers are empowered to list and manage their produce, ensuring fair pricing and quick payments. Merchants can easily browse the available stock, place orders, and track their deliveries without unnecessary delays. At the same time, administrators oversee the platform’s operations to guarantee smooth transactions, transparency, and dispute resolution, making the entire system reliable and trustworthy.

This project is a web-based marketplace designed to automate agriculture trade and bring efficiency to the sector. The development process began with a detailed feasibility study that examined the system’s practicality across several aspects. Operational feasibility was ensured by simplifying the agriculture trade process, thereby reducing manual workload and minimizing errors. Technical feasibility was achieved by implementing the platform using Java with Spring Boot, MySQL, and modern web technologies to build a robust and scalable system. The schedule feasibility ensured that the project was developed within the estimated timeframe to meet client requirements, while economic feasibility highlighted the cost-effectiveness of the solution, ensuring a high return on investment through automation and efficiency.
